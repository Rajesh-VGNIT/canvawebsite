My Story

Every single life incident, no matter how seemingly insignificant or small they are, holds the power to shape and influence our lives. Today I stand before you as a life coach and spiritual healer dedicated to helping others find their own strengths and potential. But when I look back on my life, I can clearly see how my life was full of misery, how my childhood experiences shattered my self-worth, filled me with shame, guilt, and fear, and completely broke my self image. Which lead to self-doubt, making me question my worthiness of love, success, and happiness. This belief deeply affected various aspects of my life.

Throughout my life, I faced struggles with my relationships, career, and health. I worked hard to get a job. But when found one, I wasn’t happy at all. I changed my job thrice seeking happiness and satisfaction. Then I realized that happiness doesn’t reside in external things, it blossoms from within. I was also struggling in my relationships as I wasn’t able to communicate my thoughts, feelings, and needs properly or clearly to anyone.

But one realization changed the trajectory of my life. My broken self-image and unresolved frustrations were not only affecting me but also impacting those closest to me, including my own daughters.

I saw how my own struggles and insecurities were unintentionally shaping my daughter’s perception of herself and the world around her.

Then, I embarked on a journey to not only heal myself but also to create a nurturing environment for her.

I immersed myself in various methods, exploring different healing modalities. It was a process of deep introspection, reflection, and learning as I sought to understand my own wounds and find the tools to heal them. It was through the ancient wisdom of chakras and the support of my mentor I started learning the key life skills for living a fulfilled life. The symbolism of the tarot became the gateway of my subconscious providing me insights and guidance when I needed them the most. The tarot becomes a mirror reflecting my inner struggles and untapped potential. In this voyage of self-discovery, I not only found a deeper understanding of myself but also witness the world around me through a new lens of wisdom and wonder.

This wisdom healed my wounds and open the doors of a completely new life. The connection I lost with myself built up again. I discover the untapped potential within me. I outgrew my past and started appreciating myself and the beauty within. I started to connect with my higher self.
Now, my story has brought me to a pivotal moment – a moment of readiness to extend my hand to those seeking guidance and support. With a heart overflowing with compassion and a profound understanding of the human spirit, I am now stepping into the role of a healer and guide to others.
As I embrace this new chapter, my mission is clear: to support and uplift those in need, just as I once yearned for guidance and transformation. Together, we will navigate life’s complexities, uncover hidden strengths and create a fulfilling future filled with self-love, resilience, and purpose.
Throughout this transformative journey, I stand with firm dedication and a deep commitment to helping others unlock the doors to their own healing and self-discovery. Each step I take in my life’s unfolding story fills me with gratitude, knowing I have the opportunity to be a guiding light for others, illuminating their paths to growth, healing, and endless possibilities.
